## 中文简介

Steam 卡牌助手是一款 Tampermonkey 用户脚本，可扫描未完成的徽章，批量查询卡牌价格，估算补全与升至五级的成本，并自动填写 Steam multibuy 或批量提交长期订购单。支持价格上限、提前预测、三种订购价格、买价调整和黑名单管理。在卡牌价格不断上涨的情况下，更快找到合适的徽章，少花时间，也尽量少花钱。

## Greasy Fork 中文

Steam 卡牌助手用于扫描未完成的 Steam 徽章并批量查询卡牌价格。它可以计算补全一套、完整一套和升至五级的预计成本，提前跳过明显超出预算的卡组，并自动填写 multibuy 的数量和价格。

扫描结果支持多选、重新计算和长期订购单；订购价格可使用在售最低、平均价格或求购最高。脚本还提供自动黑名单、固定黑名单、价格缺失估算和可调请求冷却。

适合想更快筛选低成本徽章、减少重复操作，并在卡牌越来越贵时更好控制预算的用户。

## English Description

Steam Trading Card Helper is a Tampermonkey userscript that scans unfinished Steam badges, checks card prices in bulk, and estimates the cost of completing a set or reaching level five.

It can skip clearly overpriced sets early, fill Steam multibuy quantities and prices, recalculate owned cards, and submit long-term buy orders using the lowest sell price, median price, or highest buy order. It also includes price adjustments, existing-order deductions, and blacklist management.

Built to save time and help control badge costs as trading cards become more expensive.

## Reddit Short Post

I made a Tampermonkey userscript called Steam Trading Card Helper. It scans unfinished badges, checks card prices in bulk, estimates set and level-five costs, and can fill multibuy or prepare long-term buy orders.

It also supports budget limits, early skipping for expensive sets, price adjustments, and blacklists. The goal is simple: spend less time opening market pages and keep badge costs under control.
